Game Carton

This is the "GameCarton," our made-up company for a graphic design project. The idea was a game console, but in the shape of a milk carton because it seemed funny. The name was styled after "xbox" or "playstation," where there's a theme of thing + location. An "X" box, a "play" station, etc. The first image is the logo, the second is the poster we printed, and the third is a fun 3D render I did. I did the 3D render because I wanted an excuse to learn Blender, and a milk carton felt like a good, easy yet recognizable shape to design. Also I thought that saying I had a raytraced milk carton would be kind of funny.
