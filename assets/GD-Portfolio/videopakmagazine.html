Magazine


Here's a magazine I made about a cool project I worked on over last summer. This is about Videopaks, which are little animations or utilities that sync up with music you're making on the synthesizer known as the "OP-Z." I've made a few, one that is just a simple XYpad and slider, and another one that imitates a feature on another synthesizer I like called the "OP-1."
